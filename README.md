# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurants Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard** built using the **Zomato Restaurants Dataset**. This project transforms restaurant data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, DAX measures, and AI-inspired recommendations. It enables stakeholders to evaluate restaurant performance, customer satisfaction, cuisine trends, pricing strategies, and market expansion opportunities.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

The restaurant industry generates a large volume of customer and operational data. This dashboard helps convert that data into actionable insights by analyzing restaurant distribution, customer engagement, cuisine popularity, pricing patterns, online delivery services, and strategic expansion opportunities.

The report consists of **five interactive dashboard pages**, each focusing on a different business objective while providing a complete overview of restaurant performance across multiple countries and cities.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze restaurant distribution across countries and cities.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction using ratings and customer votes.
- Identify the most popular and highest-rated cuisines.
- Compare restaurant performance across different price ranges.
- Analyze online delivery and table booking availability.
- Identify high-potential markets for future expansion.
- Generate AI-inspired business recommendations for better decision-making.

# 📑 Dashboard Pages

## 📌 Page 1 – Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of the restaurant ecosystem using KPIs and interactive visuals.

### Key KPIs

- Total Restaurants
- Countries Served
- Total Cities
- Total Cuisine Categories
- Average Rating
- Total Customer Votes
- Average Cost for Two
- Online Delivery Availability

### Visuals

- Online Delivery Availability
- Table Booking Availability
- Top Cities by Restaurant Count
- Average Restaurant Rating by Country

### Key Insight

New Delhi has the highest restaurant concentration, while only around **29%** of restaurants offer online delivery, indicating strong opportunities for service expansion.

---

## ⭐ Page 2 – Customer Experience Analytics

Analyzes customer satisfaction, restaurant popularity, pricing strategies, and customer engagement.

### Visuals

- Top Restaurants by Average Rating
- Impact of Online Delivery on Customer Ratings
- Price Range vs Rating & Customer Engagement
- Customer Ratings & Votes Across Price Ranges
- Business Questions Answered Panel

### Key Insight

Restaurants providing online delivery generally receive better customer ratings, while premium price segments attract higher customer engagement.

---

## 🍜 Page 3 – Cuisine & Market Intelligence

Analyzes cuisine popularity, customer preferences, pricing, and market trends.

### KPIs

- Countries Served
- Cuisine Categories
- Most Reviewed Cuisine
- Highest Rated Cuisine
- Most Popular Cuisine

### Visuals

- Top Rated Cuisines
- Restaurant Count & Average Rating by Cuisine
- Average Cost for Two by Cuisine
- Customer Votes by Cuisine
- AI Cuisine Insights

### Key Insight

North Indian cuisine dominates the market, while Sunda cuisine achieves the highest customer rating.

---

## 📈 Page 4 – Expansion Opportunities & Strategic Decisions

Supports business expansion by identifying high-performing cities and investment opportunities.

### KPIs

- Top Performing City
- Top Online Delivery Market
- Highest Customer Satisfaction City
- Highest Customer Engagement City
- Premium Dining Market
- Top Expansion Opportunity

### Visuals

- Average Cost for Two by City
- Top Rated Cities
- Online Delivery Penetration
- Customer Engagement by City
- Customer Engagement Driver Analysis (Decomposition Tree)

### Key Insight

New Delhi generates the highest customer engagement, while several cities present strong opportunities for future business expansion.

---

## 🤖 Page 5 – AI Restaurant Recommendation Center

Provides restaurant rankings and AI-inspired recommendations using a custom performance score.

### Visuals

- Top Restaurants by Performance Score
- Restaurant Performance Summary
- Restaurant Distribution Across Price Segments
- AI Business Recommendations

### Key Insight

The dashboard highlights high-performing restaurants, identifies restaurants requiring operational improvements, and recommends expansion strategies based on customer engagement and performance metrics.

---

# 📊 Key Business Insights

- The dataset contains **9,551 restaurants** operating across **15 countries** and **141 cities**.
- New Delhi has the highest number of restaurants and customer engagement.
- Only **29.41%** of restaurants provide online delivery.
- North Indian cuisine dominates in terms of restaurant count and customer votes.
- Sunda cuisine records the highest customer satisfaction.
- Premium restaurants generally achieve higher customer ratings.
- AI-driven recommendations help identify high-performing restaurants and strategic market opportunities.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand online delivery services in high-demand markets.
- Promote highly rated but lesser-known restaurants through targeted marketing.
- Improve customer experience in low-rated restaurants.
- Expand successful cuisine categories into emerging markets.
- Invest in premium dining locations with strong customer engagement.
- Increase adoption of table booking services to enhance customer convenience.
